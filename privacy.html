4. SMS / Text Messaging Terms

If you provide your mobile phone number through our chat widget or otherwise consent to be contacted by text, the following terms apply to text (SMS/MMS) messages from Motion Marketing:

  • Consent: By providing your mobile number and agreeing to be contacted, you consent to receive text messages from Motion Marketing related to your inquiry, such as replies to your questions, follow-ups about sales opportunities or services, scheduling, and account or service updates. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of any purchase or employment.
  • Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us.
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply, according to your mobile carrier plan.
  • Opt out: You can cancel text messages at any time by replying STOP. After you reply STOP, we will send one confirmation message and will not send you any further texts unless you opt in again.

  • Carriers: Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third parties. All other information-sharing categories described in this policy exclude text messaging opt-in data and consent.
